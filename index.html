SoyBase

SoyBase integrates genetic and genomic information to aid soybean breeders and researchers. This instance of the site has been ported to a different framework in order to accommodate the rapidly-growing genomic data available. We anticipate that that this transition will continue through 2023. In the meantime, you can also continue to use "SoyBase classic".

The site is organized as follows. See GENOMICS to access resources and tools such as browsers, InterMine instances, and other visualization tools. See DATA COLLECTIONS to access data sets for download. See TOOLS if you have particular tasks in mind.

Tools
Analysis tools for soybean: gene families and phylogenies, synteny visualization, etc. search, etc.
Maps
Soybean genetic maps, the SoyMine interface to SoyBase data, and visualization tools for exploring GWAS, QTL and diversity data.
Genomics
Genomic resources for soybean: data sets, genome browsers, pan-gene sets, synteny visualizations, etc.
Data Collections
View synopses of and links to individual SoyBase Datastore collections.
Projects
Projects associated with or contributed by the soybean research-community.
Community
Other community resources for soybean research.
About & Contact
Information about soybean data such as nomenclature, data submission. Also, a form to contact us if you have questions or suggestions!
Help & Tutorials
Videos and tutorials for working with SoyBase, and other videos about soybean.
Blog
Learn about new data and tools available from LIS.

Funding and Development

SoyBase and LIS is funded by the USDA-ARS. LIS is developed and hosted by the National Center for Genome Resources (NCGR) and the USDA-ARS SoyBase and Legume Clade Database group at Ames, IA, with the help of many other researchers.
