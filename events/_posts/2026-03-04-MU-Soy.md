<p>

The symposium will bring together researchers and stakeholders across the soybean value chain to network, present research findings, and promote interdisciplinary collaborations. It will feature four leading experts in the areas of soybean molecular genetics, weed science, food science, and soybean breeding. Participants also have the opportunity to volunteer poster presentations and discuss their work during a poster session which will be coupled with a reception.
