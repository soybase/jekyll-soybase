 Searching of an allele can be performed by entering a gene identifier and flanking regions or by selecting a chromosome and start and stop coordinates. The tool will return the position and allele status of any SNPs in the requested region. This information is important to assays of gene functional differences and to the design of markers for Marker Assisted Selection (MAS) or QTL analysis.
