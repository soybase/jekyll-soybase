SBW2026 Workshop: Phenotypic Plasticity & GxE

In this workshop, we will:

  • Phenotypic plasticity and explain two different origins of genotype-by-environment interaction.
  • Practice an open-source CERIS-JGRA package to dissect and predict quantitative traits.
  • Identifying genomic and environmental determinants underlying phenotypic plasticity.

Graduate Student Poster Competition and Student / PostDoc oral presentations

Abstracts must be submited by January 12, 2026 to be considered for the graduate student poster competition and/or the oral presentation.

  • Poster Competition:
    • The graduate student poster competition will be held in conjunction with the evening social hour.
    • Cash awards will be awarded for the 1st ($300), 2nd ($200), 3rd ($100) place winners on Wednesday morning.

  • 15-minute Oral Presentation:
    • Graduate Students and PostDocs are eligable.
    • Candidates will be notified by January 16, 2026 if they were selected for an oral presentation.
