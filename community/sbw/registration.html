New for the 2025 Soybean Breeder Workshop

This year the lighting talks will be choosen from the poster abstracts and to be considered for a talk the poster abstract must be submitted before January 10, 2025. The choosen abstarts will be asks to give a 15 minute presentation.

Poster Presentation and Judging Information

The poster session will be during the evening social hour. Anyone is welcome to display a poster. There will be a graduate student competition, sponsored by the commercial soybean breeders, during the poster session. Cash prizes will be awarded Wednesday morning. Please submit the poster abstract before January 10, 2025 to be considered in the Lighting Talk selection. Please to submit a poster abstract.

  • Anyone is welcome to display a poster. Posters previously presented at other meetings are welcome.
  • Poster display space is nominally 36 inches by 48 inches
  • Poster abstracts will be accepted until January 31, 2025
  • Posters can be put up in the morning of Monday and must be up by noon on Tuesday, in order to be considered for awards.
  • Posters must be removed by noon on Wednesday.
  • Student Posters will be judged and awards given for the 1st ($300), 2nd ($200), 3rd ($100) place winners.
  • Awards will be presented on Wednesday morning during the opening Welcome and Announcements. If you are not present, you will be notified by email and the award will be sent to you by mail.
  • For questions about the poster session please contact Cleiton Wartha
