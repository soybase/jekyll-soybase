Soybean Community Awards

The Soybean Genomics Executive Committee (SoyGEC) has established career awards for individuals conducting research impacting US soybeans. These will be award at the Biennial Cellular and Molecular Biology of the Soybean Conference. Information on how to nominate a member of the soybean community for an award can be found here.
